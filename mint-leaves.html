Mint Leaves: A Refreshing Herb

Mint leaves are a fragrant herb commonly used in culinary and medicinal applications. Known for their cooling properties, mint is often used to aid digestion and provide a refreshing flavor to drinks and dishes.

Key Benefits of Mint Leaves:

  • Helps with digestion and relieves indigestion.
  • Has a calming effect and helps relieve headaches.
  • Improves breath and has natural antibacterial properties.
  • Reduces inflammation and helps with skin conditions.
  • Boosts energy and refreshes the body.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves (washed)
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice (optional, for added flavor)
  • Fresh lemon slice (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  • Boil 1 cup of water in a kettle or saucepan.
  • Place the fresh mint leaves in a mug or teapot.
  • Once the water has boiled, pour it over the mint leaves.
  • Let the tea steep for 5-10 minutes, depending on how strong you want the flavor.
  • Remove the mint leaves from the water by straining the tea.
  • Optional: Add honey and lemon juice to sweeten and flavor the tea.
  • Garnish with a fresh lemon slice if desired.
  • Stir well and enjoy your refreshing mint tea!
