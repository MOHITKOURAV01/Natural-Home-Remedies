Clove: A Powerful Spice

Clove is a fragrant spice derived from the dried flower buds of the clove tree. It is widely used in cooking and has a variety of health benefits.

Key Benefits of Clove:

  • Rich in antioxidants, which help fight free radicals in the body.
  • Has anti-inflammatory properties that support joint health.
  • Improves digestion and reduces bloating and indigestion.
  • Helps relieve dental pain and acts as a natural antiseptic.
  • Boosts immune system function and helps fight infections.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 whole cloves
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1-2 slices of fresh ginger (optional, for added flavor)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice (optional, for extra flavor)

Instructions

  • Boil 1 cup of water in a saucepan or kettle.
  • Add 4-5 whole cloves to the boiling water.
  • Optional: Add 1-2 slices of fresh ginger to the water for an extra burst of flavor.
  • Let the cloves (and ginger, if using) steep in the water for 5-10 minutes.
  • Optional: Add honey to sweeten the tea and 1 teaspoon of lemon juice for extra flavor.
  • Strain the tea into a mug to remove the cloves (and ginger slices if used).
  • Stir the tea and enjoy your flavorful, soothing clove tea!
